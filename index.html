Learn the Basics?

Vaulture gives you one official run each day. Try a quick practice tutorial first so your first real run counts.

How to Play

The Daily Run
Everyone cracks the same vault each day. You get one official scored run.
Build a Play
Tap crew cards to add them to your play — up to 5 at once. Matching crew make bigger multipliers (×2 → ×4 → ×8 → ×15).
Check the Preview
Your combo, loot, and multiplier update before you commit.
Commit Carefully
Press Play and the hand counts for the run. Crack each lock's target before your plays run out.
Share Your Result
When the run ends, share a spoiler-free result card and compare scores.
